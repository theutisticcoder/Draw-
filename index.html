Physical Emulation Physics

Viscous Blending:
The beauty of RGB oil math.

The upgraded RGB engine calculates color values by dynamically tracking your canvas's pigment loads. It blends overlapping strokes together smoothly while preventing compounding color decay or clipping to black.

Dynamic Reservoir Pickup

As your brush moves through wet paint, it collects pigment off the canvas, continuously modifying the brush color until the loaded reservoir runs clean or is re-dipped.

localForage Integration

Bypasses standard 5MB localStorage size limits, utilizing native IndexedDB storage to persist raw, uncompressed heavy oil brush layers.
