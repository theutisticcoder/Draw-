The Canvas Shader Technology

Standard digital drawing software stacks pixels flatly over pixels. L'Atelier's Wet Paint simulator calculates a displacement map overlay based on paint deposit height.

3D Bump Shading (Normal Mapping)

Custom SVG multi-pass emboss filter matches canvas lighting coordinates dynamically as you adjust heights.

Wet Pigment Sampling

As the brush slides across a colored background, the algorithm samples and dilutes current ink colors with newly gathered pigments.
