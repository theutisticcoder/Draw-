Viscous Blending:
The beauty of RGB oil math.

The upgraded engine calculates color values by dynamically tracking your canvas's pigment loads. It blends overlapping strokes together smoothly while preventing compounding color decay or clipping to black.

Dynamic Reservoir Pickup

As your brush moves through wet paint, it collects pigment off the canvas, continuously modifying the brush color until the loaded reservoir runs clean or is re-dipped.

Heightmap Impasto (WebGL)

Every bristle dab generates a micro height-map. A custom GLSL shader calculates directional normals in real-time to cast physical shadows and specular highlights on the wet paint ridges.
